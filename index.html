Initials-ware

Remember your password with initials of people you know.

Passwords should be strong. An entropy of 64 bits is given by a 14-letter all-lowercase password.

Passwords should be easy to remember (if you need to type them yourself). By giving you 7 known* people having the initials of your password, you get an inspiration for your mnemonic. However, feel free to use other people, e.g. from your contacts, if you can remember them better.

* Don't know these people? Help me to find better ones.
